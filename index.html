Hi everyone!

We are releasing a new version of Mesh.

Responsive Mesh

Mesh is now simpler than the previous version and more light. But the main improvement is that Mesh is responsive.

Let’s take a look at how the expand-umns will be sized. The left image shows you the desktop size, and the right side shows you how it will be displayed in a devices with less than 480px.
